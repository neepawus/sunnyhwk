ty explained

This is a special sound that does not exist in English at all. It's like the sound for [t] and [k] but smooshed together. The result means your taking the flat part of your tongue and making it touch the roof of you mouth very quickly. It's normal to feel weird when pronouncing it

Notes

q

A q anywhere in a word except if it is the last letter is pronounced as in the chart above. A q in final position (the last letter in the word) is pronounced as the k above.

uy

Whenever the sounds [u] and [y]combine, we hear the sound [ee].

uw

Whenever the sounds [u]and [w] combine, we heard the sound [8]
