Sunny Ôchumuk

about

sunny is a two spirit, non-binary, black & wôpanâak writer, violinist, and community organizer. they believe that stories are mediums for self-expression, connecting with others, and passing on important information. they hope that their stories will illustrate a different and less stereotypical side of contemporary indigenous life.
